Case Study
4D Molecular Therapeutics

Evaluating and selecting an AI partner for BLA readiness

4D Molecular Therapeutics (4DMT) is a clinical-stage gene therapy company developing treatments for retinal and pulmonary diseases. With its lead candidate advancing toward a Biologics License Application (BLA), the company set an ambitious goal: BLA submission within 3 months of database lock. Traditional CSR first draft generation alone takes 4 to 6 weeks, and the team had no dedicated medical writer. 4DMT needed an AI partner that could deliver at the speed and quality the submission demanded.

Challenge

  • An aggressive timeline with a lean team. BLA preparation spans dozens of documents, including CSRs, Module 2 summaries, and patient narratives. Without a medical writer on staff, 4DMT needed a platform that could produce high-quality first drafts at scale.
  • A crowded vendor landscape with uneven capabilities. Multiple vendors offered AI-powered content generation for regulatory submissions, but their actual capabilities varied widely. Without a standardized way to compare them, selecting the wrong partner carried real risk.
  • Non-negotiable compliance requirements. Any solution would need to satisfy enterprise-grade requirements for data encryption, hosting, system integration, and GxP compliance before it could touch submission-critical content.

The Approach

4DMT assembled a cross-functional team: regulatory affairs defined the evaluation strategy and scoring criteria, project management coordinated the process, and IT and GxP specialists assessed each vendor's security and compliance posture.

Why 4DMT Selected Peer AI

The scoring model identified Peer AI as the top-performing vendor across evaluation criteria.

  • Consistent quality leadership. Peer AI scored highest or tied for highest across accuracy, completeness, readability, consistency, and other quality metrics evaluated by the independent reviewers.
  • End-to-end workflow fit. Source documents went in; a first draft CSR came out, with source data verified, formatting aligned to the client template, and summaries drafted. That direct path fit the team's lean operating model.
  • Enterprise compliance cleared. Peer AI's responses to IT and GxP questionnaires satisfied 4DMT's requirements across data encryption, secure hosting, and system integration.
  • Collaborative partnership through the pilot. The Peer AI team worked closely with 4DMT to understand specific needs and refine outputs, building confidence the relationship would scale beyond the evaluation.

"We performed side-by-side comparisons of AI-generated outputs versus human-written CSRs. We developed a robust QC checklist to evaluate formatting, accuracy, readability, and redundancy, and translated findings into a data-driven scoring model. This approach enabled us to successfully identify Peer AI as the optimal partner to support our BLA preparation."

Catherine Campbell
VP Regulatory Affairs, 4D Molecular Therapeutics

Human in the Loop

4DMT was clear-eyed about where AI adds value and where human expertise remains essential. The team's framework placed AI in the domain of automation, execution, scale, and speed. Judgment, higher-order communication, key messaging, ethics, decision-making, and verification stayed with the human team. This distinction shaped how vendors were evaluated and how 4DMT plans to integrate AI into its submission workflow.

Looking Ahead

With Peer AI selected as the company's AI partner for regulatory content, 4DMT is positioned to accelerate its BLA preparation timeline, targeting submission within 3 months of database lock. The team expects cost savings of more than 50% and time savings of more than 80% compared with traditional authoring approaches, while maintaining comparable quality.

The structured evaluation process also serves as a reusable framework. As 4DMT builds internal governance around AI use, the scoring rubric, QC checklist, and cross-functional review model the team developed can guide future technology decisions across the organization and serve as a reference for other companies navigating the growing landscape of AI vendors for regulated content.
